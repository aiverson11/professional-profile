Advocacy

As someone who actively joins mission trips, I’ve seen how meaningful change begins with compassion and connection. These experiences have inspired me to extend my mission beyond physical outreach and into the digital world. My advocacy focuses on promoting digital literacy, especially among students and young people in underprivileged communities.

In many places I’ve visited, access to technology is growing, but awareness of how to use it safely and effectively remains limited. Through workshops, mentorship, and online campaigns, I aim to teach practical digital skills, online safety, and ethical use of technology. I believe that by educating others on how to use digital tools wisely, we can open doors to new learning opportunities and empower individuals to become responsible digital citizens.

By combining my passion for service with technology, I hope to bridge the gap between communities and the digital world. This advocacy is not just about teaching skills—it’s about inspiring people to use technology as a means to uplift others, strengthen communities, and continue the spirit of mission work in a modern, connected way.
